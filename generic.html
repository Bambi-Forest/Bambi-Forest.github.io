About Me

A career changer.....with Passion for Data

Nage Murphy is a Junior Data Analyst, with experience collecting data, cleaning the data, merging everything together, following up with the "client", and not getting bogged down in the details. Her background in healthcare of 5 years as a Clinical Laboratory Scientist enhances her ability in time management, strong decision-making skills, patience and learning quickly. Nage is fueled by her passion for understanding data to get a result that drives an informed decision. She considers herself a ‘forever student’ eager to both build on her experience as a new junior data analyst and stay in tune with the latest data analytic technologies through continued coursework.

Her hunger and determination to turn messy data into action has contributed to her most recent success at South Louisiana Community College, where she gathered data and produced visualizations for clients. Meanwhile, she vastly improved the productivity of her team by taking on the backlog work and ensuring confidentiality for her clients.

Nage believes in working smarter to reach her goals is key to success – a tenet she lives out through her interest in learning data science outside of her academic curriculum, learning python on her free time, and creating projects to gain better understanding. Nage is currently attending college while working as a junior data analyst part-time and is always interested in new opportunities that suits her work-life balance needs to enjoy her family.
